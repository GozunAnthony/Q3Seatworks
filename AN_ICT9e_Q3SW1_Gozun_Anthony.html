Cobra Kai

Thirty four years after events of the 1984 All Valley Karate Tournament, a down-and-out Johnny Lawrence seeks redemption by reopening the infamous Cobra Kai dojo, reigniting his rivalry with a now successful Daniel LaRusso.

Characters:

MIGUEL DIAZ

Miguel Diaz is one of the main characters in Cobra Kai. Miguel is the neighbor of former karate champion Johnny Lawrence, who convinces Johnny to train him. Miguel helps him to reopen and establish a student base at Cobra Kai Dojo, though he quickly falls into the Cobra Kai mentality of "no mercy".

Johnny Lawrence

Johnny Lawrence is the secondary antagonist of The Karate Kid and one of the main protagonists in the Cobra Kai television series

Daniel LaRusso

Daniel LaRusso is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the The Karate Kid franchise. He is the protagonist of The Karate Kid's film trilogy and the main protagonist in its spin-off series Cobra Kai.

Eli Moskowitz/Hawk

Eli Moskowitz better known as Hawk is a central character and the tritagonist of the YouTube Red/Netflix series, Cobra Kai.

Robby Keene

Robby is the son of karate sensei Johnny Lawrence and alcoholic Shannon Keene. Initially a troublemaker that broke the rules, Robby began to change his ways when Daniel LaRusso entered his life. He learned discipline, acceptance, and to control the anger within him. He became the first disciple of the new generation of Miyagi-Do Karate and had a blossoming relationship with Daniel's daughter, Sam. His feelings for her led to a bloody rivalry with Miguel Diaz, which came to a head during a brawl at West Valley High.
